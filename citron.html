What is Citron Tea?

Korean citron tea is made with exotic yuja fruit. The tea has a distinctive aroma and is delicious and comforting. A perfect drink for winter. A herbal tea made with yuja-cheong (유자청) aka yuja citron marmalade. Typically, yuja-cheong is made with yuja fruit, sugar and/or honey at 1 to 1 ratio.
