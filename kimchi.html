What is Kimchi Stew?

Made with kimchi and other ingredients, such as pork or seafood, scallions, onions, and diced dubu. It is one of the most common stews in Korean cuisine. Kimchi jjigae is often cooked using older, more fermented, and "riper" kimchi, which has a much more pronounced flavor
