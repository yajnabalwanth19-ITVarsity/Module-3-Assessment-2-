Bibimbap 비빔밥

Bibimbap is served as a bowl of warm white rice topped with namul (sautéed and seasoned vegetables) or kimchi (traditional fermented vegetables) and gochujang (chili pepper paste), soy sauce, or doenjang (a fermented soybean paste).

Soft Tofu Soup 순두부찌개

Sundubu-jjigae or soft tofu stew is a jjigae in Korean cuisine. The dish is made with freshly curdled soft tofu, vegetables, sometimes mushrooms, onion, optional seafood, optional meat, and gochujang or gochu garu.

Kimchi Stew 김치찌개

Kimchi-jjigae or kimchi stew is made with kimchi and other ingredients, such as pork or seafood, scallions, onions, and diced dubu.
