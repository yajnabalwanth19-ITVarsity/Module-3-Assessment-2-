What is Bunggeo-ppang?

Fish-shaped pastry stuffed with sweetened red bean paste.The snack is often sold at street stalls, grilled on an appliance similar to a waffle iron but with a fish-shaped mold. Red bean paste is the standard filling but many bungeo-ppang sold as street food are filled with pastry cream
